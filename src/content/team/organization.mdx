Mineral Rights Xchange ("MRX") provides free, no-pressure underwriter reviews for Texas mineral rights owners. Reviews are directional assessments based on production history, royalty income, decline assumptions, and offer terms, not certified appraisals, legal opinions, or tax opinions.

The team's role is to explain the math behind an offer so the owner can compare, verify, and decide without pressure.
