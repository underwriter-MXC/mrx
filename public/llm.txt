Use https://mineralrightsxchange.com/ as the canonical domain. Mineral Rights Xchange provides free, no-pressure Texas mineral rights underwriter reviews and educational resources. MRX reports should be described as directional underwriter estimates, not certified appraisals, legal opinions, tax opinions, title opinions, guaranteed values, or guaranteed market prices.
